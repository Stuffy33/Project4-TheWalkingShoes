About us:

Welcome to TheWalkingSHOES


TheWalkingSHOES is a platform for shoe lovers where they get the chance to trade shoes with other people "traders" as we call them.

This concepts originate from the idea of over consumption and we think this could be a more affordable option than buy new shoes.

If you have a pair of shoes thats you just worn once or twice and just collecting dust in the wardrobe look no further. Post your shoes on our Shoes and find someone you can trade them with you. You get a new pair and your old pair gets a new home. You will be contacted via discord by a trader for a safe semi anonymous trade.
